The more secure you are, the more convenient your life becomes!

In "Becoming Anonymous: The Complete Guide To Maximum Security Online" a Youtube video, created by Techlore, identifies the steps you need to take in order to become "anonymous" and protect your privacy. Our privacy is constantly being invaded by sites we visit, our ISP, the government, services we use online (social media), who sell your data to third party marketers. Your data is circulating throughout the internet, and unless you take action in protecting your sensitive information, it will live online forever!
