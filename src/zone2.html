Your Smartphone

The ongoing debate on which is better, Iphone or Android can be established today! Iphone is the most secure device while Android devices are more likely to get infiltrated because they do not get regular security updates. However, if you have a stock Android device it does get frequent updates and it is a much better option.

Storage

You need to periodically wipe your hard drives to free up space and you can do so by using a tool like CCleaner so you cannot recover any of the files you decide to delete. Another key element in storage is encryption, you should be using drive encryption and you should never remove the encryption from any device, especially android, if you are given the option to do so.

Your Web Browser

You should avoid web browsers like chrome because Google has been known to collect your data. Web browsers like firefox or any other privacy based browser to keep your privacy secure. In terms of extension you should use an ad blocker such as uBlock Origin, a tracker blocker such as Privacy Badger, and HTTPS Everywhere to keep you using HTTPS instead of HTTP. Ensure flash and JavaScript are also disabled in your browser.
