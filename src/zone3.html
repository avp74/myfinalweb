Zone 3 - Drastic Privacy

If you are looking to dive deeper into really securing yourself zone 3, will further guide you into how you can achieve your desire to reach a high level of privacy!

Passwords

At this point you should have a complex password to have access to your operating system. These passwords will protect your bios and storage drives so no one can have access to your operating system without having to type out the password.

Ditching Google & Your Social Media

One of the largest data collectors. It is convenient because it has everything in one location which makes it difficult to stop using. You will have to delete everything including your google account. You will also have to get rid of all your social media because it puts your privacy in danger.

Using a New Web Browser

Switching over to a web broswer like "Tor Browser", will encrypt your traffic and mask your IP address. You will have to get rid of any other browser you are using. To connect your android devices to your tor browser you can download apps such as "Orbot" and "Orfox" to connect to your network and browser.
